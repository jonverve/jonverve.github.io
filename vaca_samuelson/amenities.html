    On our many miles of land we also house two four star dining experiances, Grove Garden, and Amoura.

    Grove Garden is an italian style resturant founded by our owners husband, Paul Amour after he traveled through Italy and became enspire by their cusine. From thin crust pizza, to fresh pasta, Grove Garden offers the finest of authentic italian. Our main chief, Luca Imani, comes straight from italian and brings his vast knowledge of food straight to your table and mouths.

    Amoura is a homesyle french cuisine, that cerves authentic and home grown french food, ranging from french Coq Au Vin, and Escorgot to Gougere and Jambon Persille. Made with only fresh veggies (of which many are grown right here), and fresh seafood, we are able to bring a new light to old French classics.We are rpoud to anounce that Amoura has a new lead cheif, Joseph Moreau, who has won three awards worldwide for his outstanding cooking.

    For more information, or to look at the menu's of either of these resturant's, visit their respective websites.     AVEC HOTEL is the right choice for visitors who are searching for a combination of charm, peace and quiet, and a convenient position from which to explore France. It is a small, comfortable hotel, situated on the Canale di Cannaregio. The Avec family and their staff offer an attentive, personalized service and are always available to offer any help to guests.

    The hotel is arranged on five floors, with a lift. On the ground floor, apart from the reception, there is a comfortable lounge where you can sit and drink tea, or just read. There is also a splendid terrace, where, you can relax and immerge yourself from morning onwards in the atmosphere of French daily life, watching the farmland, the water and the vast vineyard. Just outback of the mainfloor is our ourdoor deck with a large pool, hot tub and outdoor bar. The hotel is also one of the entries to the gold course and hiking trails.

    The rooms are arranged on all floors. On the top floor, there is also a delightful terrace or solarium available for the use of guests, from where you can enjoy the wonderful view. The buffet breakfast is served in the lounge on the ground floor, and also outside on our little patio during the summer months. The hotel provides an internet point, and a Wi-Fi service.

    Day Creek Golf Clubis a Peter Jacobsen/Jim Hardy designed course. The course is scenically designed and offers spectacular views of the vineyard. The golf course is laid out over 120 acres of land with old-growth Douglas Firs, lakes, four wetlands, and forty-three bunkers. The grounds are beautifully groomed by our experienced maintenance crew, headed by Superintendent, Mike Turley.We have a full 18 hole course, along with long range putting fields.

    Day Creek Hiking Paths is a beautiful coastal mountain that is home to many rare and interesting species. You are welcome to use the trails to explore this uncommon forest where northern and southern species meet. We have a total of three trails, 3 miles long, 5 miles long and 10 miles long that get tougher depending on the distance traveled. Each trail has midway end points nearby to our water ferry that will take you back to the hotel, main building and parking.
