    Year Round Events

    At Avec Amour we have thirty to fourty various events a year. They range from wine tastings to make your own wine workshops. Despite out focus being on wine, at least half of our events are open to the whole family, such as festivals, resturant events and game days that we host here on our vineyard. Come and explore our 30 acres, on a hike, or have a restful dinner under the stars. We have a continueous list of events that we have multiple times a year, so if you miss one, don't worry, that event will be back! Below is a list of some event we tend to have! If you want the list of currently upcoming events just scroll down!

    Starlight Wine: A late night get away, into our lovely stargazing zone. Since we are so far away from the city, we have a perfect veiw of the stars. Now you can enjoy wine while you enjoy the stars.

    Vineyard Hikes: With a vast 30 acres , of witch 10 acres run into moutian paths, we offer great hike courses for anyone wanting to both hike and take in the view of the vineyard!

    Wine Festival: This is the largest event we have, that runs twice a year! Bring your whole family to experiance rides, vendors from all over and a specialized wine drinking experiance.

    Wine Tastings: You can always set up your own personaly tasting though us, however we do have large group tastings once a month where you get a chance to taste new never before seen wines!

    Vineyard Tours: We have many vineyard tours ever couple of weekends. So come and join us to find out how our wine is made and just home much work goes into making it perfect for you!

    And so Much More... Many other unique event take place over the year so check in to see if we have anything new!
