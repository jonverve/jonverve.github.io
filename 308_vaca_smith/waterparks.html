REDWOOD LODGE WATER ATTRACTIONS

REDWOOD WATER JUNGLE (INDOOR)

Our indoor Redwood Water Jungle is for kids and adults alike ages 3+. Splash, slide, and navigate your way through our huge indoor water park full of fun things to explore. Walk under raining mushrooms, and get splashed by our giant water bucket that drops every 15 minutes!

* All children under age 6 must
be accompanied by an adult.

SPLASH ZONE (INDOOR)

Splash zone is for your kiddos aged 2-5 to come splash around and have fun. There are a multitude of toys and safe jungle gyms for them to play on. You kids with love their own little designated area that is safe and loads of fun

* All children in the Splash Zone must
be accompanied by an adult.

REDWOOD WATER JUNGLE (OUTDOOR)

Much like our indoor Water Jungle, our outdoor Water Jungle has a splash park, and a giant water bucket. We also have 6 different waterslides for you to try out. Start out easy and work your way up to our Terrible Twister slide! Guests ages 3+ are welcome!

* All children under age 6 must
be accompanied by an adult.

LAZY RIVER (OUTDOOR)

Guests aged 4+ sit back and relax as you peacefully float down our lazy river. Perched in a large inner tube, you will release any stress of life and float away to paradise.

SCUBA DIVING IN THE RIVER (OUTDOOR)

Our more adventurous guests aged 16+ will get to explore the beautiful waters of Redwood and discover the species that live in them. You will be led by a professional, certified scuba diver to help you along your journey.
