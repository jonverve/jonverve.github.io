TREE TOP OUTDOOR OBSTACLE COURSE

Do you like adventures? Do you seek thrill? Redwood Lodge's Tree Top Outdoor Obstacle Course will give you thrill and an adventure of a life time, on our three levels of courses. We also have a special Kiddie Kourse for the kidos 9 years old and younger. So come one out, face your fears, and spend a few hours in the trees!







INDOOR & OUTDOOR WATERPARKS

Swimming is a fun activity for any age, time, or season. At Redwood Lodge we have a variety of waterparks for guests of all ages. During our Spring and Summer season, enjoy our outdoor waterparks which inclue a water slide park, lazy river, splash park, and our water jungle. For our guests staying with us in the winter and colder weather, they will have a blast in our huge indoor waterpark which is open all year around. We also offer scuba diving sessions in the river.
